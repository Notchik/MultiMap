The village of Tyukhinichi is located 2 km north of the northern outskirts of the city of Brest, from which it is separated by the valley of the River Lesnaya. The river itself flows along the eastern and southern outskirts of the village. From the west adjoins the village of Staroye Selo, from the north - Koverdyaki. Tyukhinichi is an important part of the avia-mobile roads, near the village from the northern Brest semi-ring, the P16 highway branches off. With her in the village crosses the local road H-456 Skoki-Tyukhinichi-Vistychi. Three kilometers west of the village is the railway platform Pribuzhye (Belostok-Brest line)
