The village is 10 km north-east of Brest city center. Nearby are the villages of Bratylovo (in the north-east) and Malye Kosichi (in the south-west). The railway line Brest-Zhabinka passes through the village, but there is no railway platform in the village, the nearest platform in the village of Koshelevo is two kilometers from Bolshik Kosich. Nearby is also the submerged Kosichi quarry. The area belongs to the Vistula basin, around the village there is a network of reclamation ditches with a drain into the Mukhavets river. A kilometer to the south of the village is the M1 motorway (the northern semi-circle around Brest)
