Skoki is a station of the High-Lithuanian section of the Belarusian Railway in Brest .

The station is located near the northern half-circle. From the south-west of the rest is the area of ??individual development of Kozlovichi, from the southeast of it is the Katin Bor microdistrict and from the north-east of the station there is the agrarian town of Kleiniki.

The station is located between the stations of Motykaly and Brest North .

At the station there are 3 ways. The first and second, if you count from the pavilion, there is a passenger island low platform. The main way is the third one, non-stop skipping of trains is made on it.

Electric trains Brest Center - High-Litovsk stop at the station.
