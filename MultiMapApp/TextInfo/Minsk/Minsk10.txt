Pervomaisky district was founded in 1969, [5] in the present borders since 1977.

Borders
There is an area in the north-eastern part of the city between the streets of Kolas and the massifs of the Botanical Garden and the Park. Chelyuskintsev.

Major highways
Independence Avenue, st. J. Kolas Str. K. Kalinovsky, the Volgograd, Logoysky tract.

Area and population
The area is 2640 hectares, including about 500 hectares of green plantations, 10 hectares of water surface. The population is 213.9 thousand people (2009). [6]
