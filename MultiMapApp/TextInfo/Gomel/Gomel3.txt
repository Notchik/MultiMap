Novobelitsky district - administrative-territorial unit (urban area) in the city of Gomel. It is located, unlike the rest of the city, on the left bank of the Sozh River, in the south-eastern part of the city. In the Novobelitsk district, 50,000 people live (2005), which is about 10% of the population of Gomel; the area of ??the district is more than 2 thousand hectares. The main street of the district is Ilyicha Street, stretched from the north to the south, starting immediately after the automobile bridge across the Sozh River and passing to the Chernigov highway.

The head of the administration of Novobelitsky district Aleksei N. Neverov.
