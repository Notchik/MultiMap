It was founded in the early 1920s by settlers from neighboring villages. In 1930, the residents joined the collective farm. The rapid growth of the village is due to the proximity of the city of Gomel, in which locals work. The center of collective agricultural enterprise "Kozyrkov". There is a secondary school, the Palace of Culture, a library, a nursery school, an outpatient clinic, a communications office, a canteen, a sewing and shoemaking workshop, 2 stores.
